Tuscany Bike Tour (Biking Europe)

On the Tuscany Self Guided Bike Tour explore the heart of Tuscany by bike from Lucca to the famous leaning Tower of Pisa and Leonardo da Vinci’s birthplace. Ride through the Chianti’s vineyards and pedal back to the fortified town of Lucca. • Pedal through the most beautiful vineyards of the Chianti region.

Cycling in Tuscany will live up to all your dreams. Bike on quiet roads over rolling hills through the heart of Tuscany. Perhaps stopping for a long lunch at a local vineyard, medieval town or just a roadside field where you can kick back and enjoy an afternoon snooze. Gorgeous golden hillsides, rich artistic heritage, gentle biking paths and great food and wine at the end of a day’s cycing make this holiday a perfect mix of what makes Tuscany great.
